How to Book Your Safari

Provide the following information in whatapp :

  • Where you want to book a safari
  • How many persons want to go on safari
  • Call the phone number to discuss the fee and confirm safari.

    • Rajaji National Park Gates

    Chilla

    Chilla Gate offers abundant elephant sightings, dense forests, tranquil rivers, bird watching, and serene landscapes for nature enthusiasts.

    Motichur

    Motichur Gate offers excellent tiger sightings, lush forests, abundant wildlife, scenic trails, and a thrilling adventure for nature enthusiasts.

    Jhilmil Jheel

    Jheelmil Jheel is known for its aquatic birds, wetland habitats, rich vegetation, peaceful atmosphere, and excellent bird-watching opportunities.

    Ranipur

    Ranipur Gate is perfect for bird watchers, serene environments, smaller wildlife, tranquil hikes, and enjoying undisturbed nature.

    Chillawali

    Chillawali Gate provides access to diverse flora, vibrant fauna, peaceful surroundings, bird watching, and rich biodiversity for visitors.
