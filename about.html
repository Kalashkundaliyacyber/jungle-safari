About Us

Rajaji National Park



Rajaji National Park, located in the northern state of Uttarakhand, India, is a haven of natural beauty and biodiversity.
Spanning over 820 square kilometers, the park encompasses the lush Shivalik foothills and the verdant landscapes of the Himalayan ranges.
Its diverse terrain, including dense forests, grassy meadows, and meandering rivers, creates a picturesque setting that captivates nature enthusiasts.
The park is renowned for its thriving population of Asian elephants, tigers, leopards, and a myriad of bird species, making it a prime destination for wildlife lovers and birdwatchers. Additionally, Rajaji National Park's rich flora and fauna, coupled with its accessibility and well-maintained trails, offer an unparalleled opportunity for eco-tourism and outdoor adventures. Visitors can immerse themselves in the tranquility of nature, experience thrilling wildlife safaris, and enjoy the serene ambiance that this remarkable park provides.

About Us

I am Sanjay Pasard, a dedicated guide with over 25 years of experience immersing myself in the wonders of nature. My journey began with a deep-rooted passion for wildlife and has evolved into a lifelong commitment to sharing the beauty and mysteries of the natural world with others.
Throughout my career, I have specialized in offering unique and personalized experiences through jungle safaris, birdwatching expeditions, and camping adventures. My expertise allows me to craft tailor-made packages that cater to the specific interests and desires of each adventurer. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of wildlife behavior, I ensure that every journey is both safe and exhilarating.
To enhance the safari experience, I provide a well-equipped jeep along with a skilled driver, ensuring that my clients can explore the wilderness comfortably and securely. My goal is to create unforgettable memories, whether it's witnessing the majesty of a tiger in its natural habitat or listening to the symphony of birds at dawn.
Join me on a journey into the heart of nature, where every moment is an opportunity to connect with the wild. Together, we will explore, discover, and cherish the incredible beauty that our national park has to offer.
