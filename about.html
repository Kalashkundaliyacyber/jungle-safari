About Us

Team The Jungle Safari

Varun Chopra has been traveling to the jungles for the past several years. Having worked with various big names in the real estate like DLF, Emaar, JLL, and Tanden, Varun chose to follow his passion of wildlife and photography over the corporate life. He is an avid wildlife/nature photographer and conservationist. With a keen interest in wildlife and nature conservation, Varun has been appointed as Honorary Wildlife Warden by the Punjab Govt.

He has traveled extensively to the Indian jungles especially to his favorite Corbett National Park, which is very much close to his heart.

Ashu Chopra loves to be in forests and is an ardent lover of nature. She is a professional brand television industry turned wildlife/nature freak, known interest in conservation. She has traveled extensively to various national parks of India.

Ashu is great host and good cook. She brings her own culinary charm and charm to this group, she manages the planning, finances and the entire logistics. She ensures that the tour experience is worth a thousand fond memories.
